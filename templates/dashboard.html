How To Read This Dashboard

Transparency Index / LTS: The main score out of 100. Higher means clearer and more verifiable reporting.

Risk Probability: Estimated chance that claims are unclear, incomplete, or heavily offset-driven.

Radar Chart: Shows which lifecycle areas are strong and weak at a glance.

Pillar Depth Matrix: Level 0-4 view of how detailed each lifecycle pillar is.

Offset Integrity Ring: Compares offset dependence and offset quality in one visual.

Claim Density Timeline: Pattern of claim confidence and impact through the report.

Volatility Index: Stability of transparency performance over periods.

Temporal Drift: Year-over-year movement by pillar. Positive means improving, negative means declining.

Why This Score: Exact score formula with bonuses and penalties.

AI Insights + Risk Drivers: Plain-language reasons behind the score and the biggest risk factors.

Claim Reasoning: Claim-by-claim explanation with rewrite guidance.

Confidence Interval: Expected score range based on quality and consistency of evidence.
